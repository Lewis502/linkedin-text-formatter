Why does this work?

LinkedIn posts strip HTML, but keep plain text. The bold/italic styles here are actually Unicode characters from the “Mathematical Alphanumeric Symbols” block, plus combining marks for underline/strike. They survive copy‑paste because they’re text.

Tip: use styling sparingly for accessibility and readability.
